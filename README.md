## Overview

The film emulation capabilities in <a href="#">Magic Bullet Suite 13</a> reflect Red Giant's deep research into the photochemical characteristics of classic film stocks and photographic processes. Magic Bullet Film recreates the specific color response curves, grain structure, halation, and cross-process characteristics of iconic film stocks with a level of accuracy that has made it a reference tool for productions seeking to recreate or evoke specific historical photographic aesthetics. The ability to apply these emulations within the editing timeline, without requiring a specialized grading application, makes film look treatment accessible at every stage of post-production from offline edit through to final delivery.

The AI-powered noise reduction in <a href="#">Magic Bullet Denoiser III</a> addresses one of the most technically challenging problems in digital video post-production — cleaning high-ISO or poorly lit footage without destroying the fine texture detail that gives images their sense of sharpness and quality. The machine learning approach analyzes the noise structure and the underlying image content independently, removing the noise component while preserving edge detail, fine texture, and the natural grain structure of properly exposed areas. This produces results that appear significantly cleaner than the source material without the artificial plastic smoothness that aggressive <a href="#">traditional noise reduction</a> produces on faces and fine detail.

The skin retouching capabilities of Cosmo provide a specifically video-oriented approach to the beauty treatment that is standard in commercial and broadcast production. Unlike photographic retouching that works on static images, video retouching must be applied consistently across hundreds of frames without flickering or inconsistency, and must be adjustable in real time during review without the iterative render cycles that Photoshop-based approaches would require. Cosmo's footage-aware smoothing and skin tone detection apply the treatment automatically across the entire clip, with manual adjustment of the degree and color range of the effect. The <a href="#">Mojo II plugin</a> completes the suite with the signature desaturated, color-pushed teal-orange treatment that has become the visual shorthand for cinematic contemporary commercial content. For video producers who want professional-quality color treatment without the overhead of a dedicated color suite, <a href="#">MagicBulletSuite13 OSX</a> provides the complete cinematic finishing toolkit.

## Key Features

- Chromatic aberration and <a href="#">lens distortion</a> correction built into the color pipeline
- Custom <a href="#">look export and sharing</a> for maintaining consistent grade across a production
- Spot color control for <a href="#">isolating and adjusting specific hues</a> in footage
- Advanced <a href="#">secondary color correction</a> with qualification and keying tools
- Grain and texture tools adding <a href="#">film-like organic texture</a> to digital footage
- LUT support importing and applying <a href="#">3D LUT files</a> from color pipelines and cameras
- Vignette and <a href="#">edge darkening</a> tools for focus-drawing compositional treatments
- Glow and <a href="#">diffusion effects</a> replicating optical and photochemical shooting techniques
- Reference monitor <a href="#">output scopes</a> including waveform, histogram, and vectorscope
- Mixed color space support handling <a href="#">log, RAW, and standard gamma</a> footage correctly
